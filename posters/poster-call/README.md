# Call for posters 

Calling all attendees to share their insights and contributions through poster presentations at the Hamlyn workshop:
“From Code to Care: Challenges, Success Stories, and Future Directions of Open-Source Development in Medical Robotics.”

We welcome innovative work from across academia, healthcare, industry, and the open-source community.

Two to four selected abstracts will receive financial support for travel and subsistence, depending on location and available budget.
We will also recognise excellence through awards for the best poster and runner-up submissions. 

Topics include, but are not limited to:

💻 Open-source software and hardware libraries/frameworks for surgical and biomedical sciences
🤖 Open-source AI solutions for surgical and medical robotics
📝 Regulatory considerations for open-source technologies in clinical sciences
🧑‍🔧 Maintenance and long-term support of biomedical software and hardware
🧑‍🔬 Integration of open-source technologies into clinical trials
💰 Open-source solutions for low- and middle-income countries
🧪 Case studies combining existing open-source software and hardware
📄 Project summaries, 🛠️ methodologies, 🔍 research findings, and 🌐 community initiatives in surgical technologies


📅 Key dates: 

Abstract submission deadline: 29 May 2026
Notification of acceptance: 2 June 2026
Poster submission deadline: 22 June 2026
Workshop: 26 June 2026
